WHO are we?

We are a Linn-Mar club affiliated with the Young America's Foundation (YAF). Our goal is to lift up conservative values and voices by promoting student action and engagement in our high school. We believe in traditional American values and free speech, both of which we aim to protect and preserve.

WHAT do we do?

We host events, discussions, and activities that encourage students to explore conservative principles and engage in meaningful conversations about freedom, traditional values, and individual rights.

WHY join us?

Joining us means becoming part of a community that values free speech and conservative principles. You will have the opportunity to participate in events, meet like-minded individuals, and make your voice heard.

Young America's Foundation

Young America's Foundation is an organization dedicated to promoting conservatism on campuses accross the country since 1960. They provide resources, support, and opportunities for students to engage in conservative activism and preserve free speech in places where that right may be threatened. The Linn-Mar Young American's For Freedom club is part of this larger movement, working to bring these values to our school community. You can click on their logo to visit the Young America's Foundation website and learn more about their mission.
